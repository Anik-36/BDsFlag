This is our national flag



Bangladesh is an independent country. As an independent country, we have a national flag. It is the symbol of our nation. Its length and breadth I am in the ratio of 10:6. It is rectangular. It may be made of cotton, linen or silk. The background of the flag is bottle green in color with a sound globe in the center. The bottle green color stands for the everlasting freshness, youth, vigor, and vitality of our nation. The sun is the source of all on earth. Hence, the sun globe signifies that our nation is continuous and everlasting. The blood-red color of the sun globe is a memorial for the endless sacrifice of our nation in the bloody struggle for liberation. We are determined to hold the prestige of the national flag up. We are proud of our country.
